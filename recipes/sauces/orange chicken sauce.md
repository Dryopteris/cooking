# Orange Chicken Sauce

## Ingredtients

### set 1
  * 1 tbsp cornstarch
  * 2 tbsp rice wine (dry vermouth is substitute)
  * ¼ cup water
  * 1 tsp sesame oil
  * 3 tbsp Say sauce
  * 10 tbsp Sugar
  * 10 tbsp white vinegar
  * zest of 1 orange
#### set 2
  * 1½ tbsp ginger minced
  * 2 tsp garlic minced
  * ½ tsp red pepper flakes

## Instructions

1. Cambine first Set of ingredients. 
1. Add ginger, garlic, and red pepper flakes to a pan and heat for 10 seconds. 
1. Add the canbined sauce to the pan and bring to a boil.
1. Turn off the heat and toss fried breaded chicken in to coat.

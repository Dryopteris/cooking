## Chocolate mugcake

### Directions
1. Mix all ingredients together in a mug
1. Cook in microvare for a little over a minute.

### Ingredients
* 1/4 cup flour
* 1/4 cup Sugar
* 2 Tbsp Cocoa powder
* 1/8 tsp baking soda
* 1/8 tsp salt
* 3 Tbsp milk
* 2 Tbsp Canola Oil
* 1 Tbsp water
* 1 tsp Vanilla

Chocolate Chips to taste

**Total time:** 15 minutes  
**Serves:** 6 to 8

## Ingredients
- 8 cups mixed greens of your choice (about 8 ounces)
- ½ cup fresh regular or Thai basil leaves, torn
- 1 crisp Asian pear or very firm regular pear, cored and thinly sliced
- ½ red onion, sliced
- 1 firm-ripe avocado, peeled and sliced
- ½ cup sliced almonds, lightly toasted
- Sesame Ginger Dressing (recipe below)

## Instructions
1. Make Dressing
1. Tear basil leaves, core and thinly slice pear, slice red onion, peel and slice avocado, toast almonds?
1. In a large salad bowl, toss together the greens, basil, pear and onion 
1. Gently toss in the dressing to taste
1. Add the avocado, and most of the almonds
1. Garnish with the remaining almonds

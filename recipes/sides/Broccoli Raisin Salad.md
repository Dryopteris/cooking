## Serves
6-8 people

## Ingredients
- 1 cup miracle whip
- ½ cup sugar
- 2 tbsp vinegar
- ½ pack of bacon 
- 1 cup sunflower seeds
- ½ cup of raisins 
- 2 bunches of fresh broccoli, cut small

## Directions
1. bake bacon at 400° for 20 minutes, until crisp 
1. cut broccoli into bite sized pieces
1. mix miracle whip, sugar, vinegar together
1. add sunflower seeds, raisins, and broccoli 
1. mix will and chill

# Irish Beef Stew
from Chelsea (CFF)

## Ingredients
* 2 tbsp olive oil
* 3 tbsp all purpose flour
* 2 lbs beef chuck (cubed)
* ¼ lb parsnip
* 1 medium sweet onion 
* 6 large potatoes 
* 2 cloves of garlic, minced
* 2 cups beef broth
* 1 6oz can tomato paste
* 1 12oz can/bottle Irish Stout beer (Guinness) 
* 1 tbsp cold water
* 1 tbsp cornstarch



## Instructions
1. peel and cut parsnips, onion, potatoes, into chunks
1. cut the beef into 1½" cubes

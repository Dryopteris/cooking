# Honey BBQ Chicken Bites

Pair with some rice & spring onions and you have one hell of a meal prep! This one is definitely making it into the rotation.

Sweet. Smoky. Unreal.

### Serves 4
## Ingredients

### Chicken bites
- 1lb chicken breast diced
- 40g/1.4oz @mingleseasoning Korean BBQ seasoning

### Egg wash and coating
- 1 large egg beaten with 1 tsp hot sauce
- 2 cups crushed cornflakes, 120g, 4.2oz

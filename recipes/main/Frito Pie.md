# Frito Pie

## Ingredients 
* 1 lb ground beef
* Taco seasoning
* ¼ - ½ cup canned tomato sauce
* 1 can of chili beans in chili sauce
* 1 bag of Fritos 
#### Additional toppings
* onion
* black olives
* sour cream
* shredded cheese
* jalapeños
* cilantro
* nacho cheese
* salsa and other sauces

## Instructions
1. Brown 1 lb ground beef in a pan while mashing it a little to get rid of big chunks. Drain grease. 
1. Add taco seasonings.
1. Add a 1/4-1/2 cup of canned tomato sauce. 
1. Add 1/2 to a full can of chili beans in chili sauce. 
1. Simmer for about 20 minutes. 
1. Serve over Fritos with whatever toppings you like.
